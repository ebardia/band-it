# Slide 5: Signal processing & opportunity discovery

## Headline
SIGNAL PROCESSING & OPPORTUNITY DISCOVERY

## Body
Band It continuously monitors information sources and identifies signals that may indicate opportunities.

Examples:

- Market changes
- Funding announcements
- New programs
- Procurement activity
- Industry events
- Organizational changes
- Competitive movement

Weak signals often become major opportunities.

The earlier you detect them, the greater the advantage.
