# Slide 6: Humans and agents working together

## Headline
HUMANS AND AGENTS WORKING TOGETHER

## Body
Band It combines human expertise with AI capabilities.

AI agents:

- Gather information
- Monitor signals
- Analyze patterns
- Prepare recommendations

Human experts:

- Apply judgment
- Make decisions
- Provide context
- Validate results

The goal is not to replace people.

The goal is to amplify their effectiveness.
