# Slide 7: Capturing SME knowledge

## Headline
CAPTURING SME KNOWLEDGE

## Body
Many organizations depend on a small number of key people.

Those individuals hold years of experience, relationships, and decision-making knowledge.

Band It helps capture and preserve that expertise.

Subject Matter Experts can:

- Train agents
- Guide workflows
- Validate recommendations
- Remain involved wherever they choose

The result is organizational knowledge that scales beyond any one individual.
