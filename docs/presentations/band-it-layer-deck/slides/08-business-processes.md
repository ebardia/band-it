# Slide 8: Plug into existing business processes

## Headline
PLUG INTO EXISTING BUSINESS PROCESSES

## Body
Organizations already have systems and workflows in place.

Band It is designed to work with them.

Examples:

- CRM systems
- Proposal systems
- Document repositories
- Spreadsheets
- Project management tools
- Databases
- Internal applications

Band It becomes an orchestration layer that connects people, processes, and AI agents.
