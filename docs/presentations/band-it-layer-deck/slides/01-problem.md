# Slide 1: The problem — opportunities are hidden in noise

## Headline
THE PROBLEM: OPPORTUNITIES ARE HIDDEN IN NOISE

## Body
Every day, organizations are surrounded by signals.

- News articles.
- Government announcements.
- Budget approvals.
- Industry changes.
- Hiring activity.
- Competitive movements.
- Customer interactions.

Most organizations never see these signals, or they see them too late.

The challenge is no longer finding information.

The challenge is identifying which signals matter and turning them into action.
