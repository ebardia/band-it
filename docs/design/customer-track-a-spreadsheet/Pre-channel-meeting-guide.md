Use with [Pre-channel-opportunity-signals.csv](./Pre-channel-opportunity-signals.csv) and [Pre-channel-example-leads.csv](./Pre-channel-example-leads.csv).

## Open the workbook

| Tab | File |
|-----|------|
| Signal playbook | `Pre-channel-opportunity-signals.csv` |
| Examples | `Pre-channel-example-leads.csv` |

Leave `customer_verdict` and `customer_why` blank on **both** tabs until the meeting.

## What to ask the customer

1. **Rows 11–12 (placeholders):** “Tell us about your last 2–3 best truckloads—before any liquidator was involved.”  
2. **Signal tab:** For each signal type (S01–S14), mark **would have chased / never works / unsure**.  
3. **Example tab:** Which historical windows (Gustafson’s, Circle, etc.) match deals you would have made?  
4. **Negatives (rows 13–15):** Confirm we should **auto-exclude** B-Stock / ReturnPro paths from discovery.

## What NOT to show

- `For-customer-review.csv`, `Reverse-logistics-home-goods.csv` (auction/broker paths)  
- `Reverse-logistics-private-warehouses.csv` (established processors)

Optional: mention Liquidation.com **only** as resale benchmark after they give you a pre-channel ask price.

## Close

> We’re not claiming 30 truckloads in a spreadsheet. We’re aligning on **what to watch for** so Band It can alert you **before** Hilco and B-Stock get the call.
