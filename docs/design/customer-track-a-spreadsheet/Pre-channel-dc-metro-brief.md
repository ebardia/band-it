## Quick open

1. Excel → Open `Pre-channel-dc-metro-leads.csv` (UTF-8).
2. Data → Filter (or Table → Insert Table with headers).
3. Filter **row_type**:
   - **LEAD** — companies and monitors (17 rows)
   - **ACTION** — this week’s 3 tasks
   - **SCRIPT** — cold-call text
   - **ALERT** — Google Alert copy-paste strings
   - **RHYTHM** — Mon / Wed / Fri schedule
   - **QUESTION** — ask Joe after he reviews LEAD rows

Fill columns **customer_would_call** and **customer_why** on LEAD rows during the meeting.
